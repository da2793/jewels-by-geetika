# Jewels by Geetika — Business Ledgers

Open these CSV files in Excel or Google Sheets.

## Files:

1. **inventory-ledger.csv** — All products, stock levels, costs, pricing
2. **orders-ledger.csv** — Every order with full details and tracking
3. **pricing-calculator.csv** — Calculate selling price from cost
4. **expenses-ledger.csv** — Track all business expenses

## How to use:

1. Open in Google Sheets (recommended) — File > Import > Upload
2. Or double-click to open in Excel

## Pricing Formula:
```
Selling Price = (Supplier Cost + Packaging + Shipping) × Markup
```
- Standard products: 4-5x markup
- Bridal/premium: 6-8x markup
- Always round to ₹X99
